Boss Fights NEW

A challenge at the end of each hour that gates the advance to the next. Read the candles, damage the boss, don't lose your hearts. Retry-friendly — losing just re-reads the lesson.

What changed

● BEFORE

  • Clearing an hour just advanced you — no milestone moment.
  • No test that you'd actually learned the hour's lesson.

● AFTER

  • Each hour ends with a themed boss (Hour 1: 🎭 The Fakeout, Hour 2: 💀 The Liquidator).
  • Boss HP vs your 3 hearts: a correct read damages the boss, a wrong one costs a heart.
  • Beat it for a big Shell + XP reward and the advance. Lose? Retry (or skip).
  • Data-driven — adding a boss for any hour is just one more entry.
Pick a boss and fight it. The Fakeout teaches reading the WHOLE candle (body + wicks); The Liquidator drills risk management.
