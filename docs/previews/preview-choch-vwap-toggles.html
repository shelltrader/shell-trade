1 · New lessons (added to the early stages)

✅ WAIT FOR THE CLOSE
  • A candle isn't final until it CLOSES — mid-candle, price can still go anywhere.
  • Confirm your rule on the close, never on a wick or a half-formed candle.
  • A break only counts if the candle CLOSES beyond the level — a wick through that closes back is a fake-out.
🔄 CHANGE OF CHARACTER
  • A BOS breaks structure WITH the trend — continuation.
  • A ChoCh is the first break AGAINST the trend — it may be flipping.
  • ChoCh = early reversal warning. BOS = the trend is still running.

Candle-close lands in Hours 1–2 (candle theory + entering a setup); ChoCh in Hour 3 (market structure). Both added to the glossary & quizzes.
