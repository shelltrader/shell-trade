Journal Full-Screen Review + Entry Points NEW

Every journal trade now opens full-screen with entry / SL / TP and a why-this-setup & what-happened breakdown. The journal is now reachable from the intermission and the trade ticket, which also shows your record on the setup.

What changed

● BEFORE

  • Journal entries showed a summary row only — no chart.
  • The full-screen trade review existed, but only for the per-hour intermission history (wiped each hour).
  • The journal was reachable only from the in-game HUD button.
  • Nothing surfaced your past record when deciding on a trade.

● AFTER

  • Each entry has a 🔍 VIEW FULL CHART button → annotated review (entry/SL/TP, OB/BOS, VWAP, why & what-happened).
  • Every logged trade now carries its chart snapshot, so even old trades replay full-screen.
  • 📓 JOURNAL button added to the intermission screen and the trade ticket.
  • The trade ticket shows a similar-trades hint: your win rate on this exact setup.
Core runner, physics, and curriculum untouched. Uses the game's existing tradeChartSVGFull() renderer — the journal entries were simply enriched to feed it.
