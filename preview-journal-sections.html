Trading Journal — 4 Sections NEW

The journal is now tabbed: Terms (glossary), Lessons (the full curriculum, reviewable any time), Trades (your complete history), and Notes (free-form, linkable to any term, lesson, or trade).

What changed

● BEFORE

  • The journal was a single list of trades.
  • No reference material — terms and lessons lived only in fleeting pop-ups.
  • Notes were tied to one trade only; no general notebook.

● AFTER

  • Terms — a 17-term glossary, always available.
  • Lessons — the entire curriculum, re-readable on demand.
  • Trades — full persistent history (every trade, any time).
  • Notes — write takeaways and link them to a term, lesson, or trade (tap the 🔗 chip to jump).
Tap the tabs. In Notes, add a note and link it — then tap its 🔗 chip to jump to the term/lesson, or open the trade full-screen.
