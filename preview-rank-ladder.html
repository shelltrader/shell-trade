Rank Ladder — Beginner → Trader NEW

A named identity that climbs as you level up — from a drifting plankton to a crowned TRADER. It layers over the existing level system; the XP bar now fills toward your next rank.

What changed

● BEFORE

  • The HUD showed a bare “LVL 3” number.
  • Leveling up popped a plain “LEVEL 3!” with no identity or sense of journey.
  • No through-line from beginner to trader.

● AFTER

  • The HUD shows your rank (🦀 CRAB) beside the level, in the rank’s colour.
  • Crossing a tier triggers a “RANK UP — CRAB!” celebration + screen flash.
  • A clear 10-tier ladder from Drifter to TRADER gives players something to chase.
Pure presentation over player.level — one rank per level, capped at TRADER. The XP bar already shows progress to the next rank, so no new meters needed.
