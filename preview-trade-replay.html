Trade Replay — watch it play out NEW

Opening a trade in the journal or at intermission now plays a mini “video”: the entry screen, then the candles reveal one by one until the trade hits its Take Profit or Stop Loss.

What changed

● BEFORE

  • The review showed a single static snapshot of the setup.
  • You couldn't see how the trade played out — only the final outcome in text.

● AFTER

  • The trade animates from entry to exit — candles reveal toward your SL / TP lines.
  • It ends on the result (✓ TAKE PROFIT or ✗ STOPPED OUT) with the P&L.
  • Controls: ▶ REPLAY to re-watch, 📊 DETAILS for the full annotated breakdown + toggles.
  • Every trade records its own path live, so the replay is the real price action.
The two examples below auto-play on load. Tap ▶ REPLAY to watch again, or 📊 DETAILS to switch to the static annotated view (with the overlay toggles).
