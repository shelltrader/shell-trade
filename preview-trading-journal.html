Trading Journal NEW

A persistent, player-facing log of every trade — with stats and editable notes. Review without replaying the game.

What changed

● BEFORE

  • Trades only updated the running win/loss counter.
  • The per-hour tradeLog was capped at 10 and wiped every hour.
  • No way for the player to review past trades or take notes.
  • Nothing survived a page reload.

● AFTER

  • A 📓 JOURNAL button (bottom-left) opens a full review screen.
  • Every trade is logged — direction, P&L, setup, grade, R:R, balance, hour.
  • Summary stats: trades, win rate, net P&L, avg win, avg loss.
  • An editable note per trade, saved instantly and persisted across reloads (localStorage).
No changes to the core runner, the trade ticket, or the curriculum. The journal reads data the game was already capturing in resolveTrade() — this is purely additive.
