Privacy Policy

Chart Quest · Last updated 1 July 2026

Chart Quest is a game that teaches you about charts and trading. This page explains, in plain words, what information we keep and how we look after it. If anything here is unclear, email us at habitsimulator@gmail.com.

Short version: You can play as a Guest and we collect nothing on our servers. If you make an account, we keep your email and your game progress so you can pick up where you left off. We never sell your information.

What we collect

  • If you make an account: your email address and a password. Your password is handled by our sign-in provider (Supabase) and stored scrambled (hashed) — we never see or store your real password.
  • Your game progress: things like your level, your in-game "shells" score, your practice-trade journal, notes and daily streak. This is only saved to our servers when you are signed in.
  • A count of visits: we count how many times the game is opened. This count has no name, email, or anything that points to you.
  • Bug reports: if you choose to send one, we keep the message you typed and basic game info to help us fix the problem.

What we do NOT collect

  • No real name, home address, or phone number.
  • No payment or bank details — the "shells" and trades in the game are pretend and have no real money value.
  • No tracking of you across other websites, and no advertising.

Guest mode

If you play as a Guest, your progress is saved only on your own device (in your browser) and is never sent to us. Clearing your browser data will remove it.

Where your information is kept

We use trusted services to run the game:

  • Supabase — stores accounts and progress. Your data is protected so that only your own signed-in account can read it.
  • Netlify — hosts the website.
  • jsDelivr — delivers part of the game's code to your browser.
  • Binance public data — we read public market prices to draw charts. We do not send any of your information to them.

How long we keep it

We keep your account and progress for as long as you have an account. If you ask us to delete your account, we remove it.

Your choices

  • You can play without an account (Guest mode).
  • You can ask us to show you, or delete, the information tied to your account by emailing habitsimulator@gmail.com.
  • You can sign out at any time from the account menu in the game.

Children and families

Chart Quest is an educational game that younger players may enjoy. We collect as little as possible and never require personal details beyond an email to save progress. Parents and guardians: if you believe your child created an account or gave us information and you want it removed, email habitsimulator@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle information, we will update this page and change the date at the top.
