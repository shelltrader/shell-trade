🐢 HOW SHELLY GOT HER SHELL

A 6-panel origin comic for the Chart Quest turtle mascot — style mockup, built with vector art to match the in-game palette. Swap in painted/illustrated panels later; layout, pacing and tone are the things to react to.

1
Long before candles and charts, Shelly was just an ordinary turtle, drifting the quiet seafloor, collecting shells one at a time.
2
One ordinary dive, she found a shell unlike any other — etched with strange green-and-red bars she'd never seen.
3
The shell wasn't a trinket — it was a map. A vision of a hidden world built entirely from candles: the Candle Realm.
4
She fused the shell into a jetpack of her own — and every loose shell on the seafloor became currency for the journey ahead.
5
Now she leaps candle to candle — dodging reversals, riding breakouts, chasing the next setup across the Candle Realm.
6
Every trader picks a path — Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and beyond. Shelly's story ends where yours begins: welcome to Chart Quest.
