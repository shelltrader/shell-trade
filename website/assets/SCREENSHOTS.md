## Tips for clean captures
- **Full-screen the game** (hide browser chrome / phone status bar if easy) — I frame them in fake "browser/phone" chrome on the site anyway.
- Grab the moment the **teaching overlay / labels are on screen** (that's what sells it).
- Higher resolution = better. Don't worry about exact pixel sizes; the site crops to fit.
- If you can only capture on a phone (portrait), still send them — I'll swap the hero to a phone frame so it still looks right. A landscape hero shot is just a nice-to-have.

## How to send them

`website/assets/shot-*.png`, re-verify in the browser, and regenerate your review-folder
screenshots.
